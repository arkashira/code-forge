## Customer Segments
- AI coding agents and developers seeking reliable skill libraries
- Software development teams looking to automate code generation
- Enterprises aiming to improve software development efficiency
- Educational institutions teaching AI and coding

## Value Propositions
- High-quality, reliable skill libraries for AI coding agents
- Automated generation of high-quality code for real-world software tasks
- Improved software development efficiency and productivity
- Reduced time and cost associated with manual coding

## Channels
- Direct sales to enterprises and educational institutions
- Online marketplaces and app stores
- Partnerships with AI and software development tools providers
- Content marketing and SEO to attract individual developers

## Customer Relationships
- Dedicated account managers for enterprise clients
- Online support and community forums for individual developers
- Regular updates and new skill library releases
- Webinars and training sessions for educational institutions

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription-based pricing model for enterprises (starting at $5,000/month or ~165,000 THB/month)
- One-time purchase for individual developers ($99 or ~3,300 THB)
- Tiered pricing based on the number of users and features required
- Revenue sharing with key partners

## Key Resources
- Proprietary skill libraries and AI models
- Development and maintenance team
- Customer support and success team
- Marketing and sales team

## Key Activities
- Continuous improvement and expansion of skill libraries
- Development and maintenance of the platform
- Customer acquisition and retention
- Partnership development and management

## Key Partners
- AI and software development tools providers
- Enterprises and educational institutions
- Cloud service providers for hosting and infrastructure
- Marketing and advertising agencies

## Cost Structure
- Salaries and benefits for development, maintenance, and support teams
- Marketing and advertising expenses
- Cloud hosting and infrastructure costs
- Research and development for new skill libraries and features
- Customer acquisition costs (starting at $1,000/customer or ~33,000 THB/customer)
