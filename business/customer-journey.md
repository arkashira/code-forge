```markdown
# Customer Journey Map

## Aware
**Trigger Event:** Developer or team lead encounters issues with AI coding agents generating low-quality or unreliable code.
**Friction Points:**
- Lack of awareness about the existence of skill libraries for AI coding agents.
- Difficulty in finding reliable solutions for improving AI code generation.
**User Emotions:** Frustration, confusion, skepticism.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Educational content (blogs, webinars) explaining the benefits of skill libraries.
- Case studies showcasing successful implementations.
**Metric:** Number of unique visitors to educational content.

## Consider
**Trigger Event:** Developer or team lead starts researching solutions to improve AI code generation.
**Friction Points:**
- Overwhelming number of options and lack of clear differentiation.
- Skepticism about the effectiveness of skill libraries.
**User Emotions:** Overwhelmed, cautious, hopeful.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Detailed product comparisons and ROI calculators.
- Free trials or demo versions of the skill library.
**Metric:** Number of downloads of product comparisons or ROI calculators.

## Try
**Trigger Event:** Developer or team lead decides to test the skill library platform.
**Friction Points:**
- Complex onboarding process.
- Limited support during the trial period.
**User Emotions:** Excited, anxious, impatient.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Streamlined onboarding with clear instructions.
- Dedicated support during the trial period.
**Metric:** Conversion rate from trial to paid subscription.

## Adopt
**Trigger Event:** Developer or team lead decides to purchase the skill library platform.
**Friction Points:**
- High initial cost.
- Integration challenges with existing tools.
**User Emotions:** Committed, relieved, optimistic.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Flexible pricing plans and discounts for long-term commitments.
- Seamless integration support and documentation.
**Metric:** Customer acquisition cost (CAC) and lifetime value (LTV).

## Expand
**Trigger Event:** Developer or team lead experiences success with the skill library platform.
**Friction Points:**
- Limited scalability options.
- Lack of advanced features for larger teams.
**User Emotions:** Satisfied, ambitious, loyal.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Upsell opportunities for advanced features and scalability options.
- Loyalty programs and exclusive access to new features.
**Metric:** Net promoter score (NPS) and customer retention rate.
```
